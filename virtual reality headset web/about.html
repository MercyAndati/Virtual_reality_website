Bigscreen Beyond: The world's best VR headset

Founded in 2018, we are a global team of developers passionate about virtual reality, based in Westland, Nairobi.

Early Developments

Located in the vibrant heart of Westland, Nairobi, our team is inspired by the rich history of virtual reality. The concept of VR has been around for decades, with one of the earliest VR headsets developed in 1968 by Ivan Sutherland. This pioneering work laid the foundation for the sophisticated VR technology we enjoy today.

Our VR Headsets

We pride ourselves on offering some of the most advanced VR headsets available. Our devices provide unparalleled immersive experiences, combining cutting-edge graphics with seamless motion tracking. Whether you're exploring virtual worlds or enhancing your professional skills, our headsets deliver exceptional clarity and comfort.

Modern Applications

We are at the forefront of integrating VR into various sectors. Our technology is used in gaming, education, healthcare, and architecture, providing immersive experiences that enhance learning, improve therapy outcomes, and allow for more accurate design visualization.

Notable Achievements

As a leading VR developers , we have collaborated with local and international partners to push the boundaries of what VR can achieve. Our commitment to innovation and quality has positioned us as a key player in the global VR community.

Community Engagement

We are dedicated to engaging with the local community through workshops and events that showcase the potential of VR. By sharing our expertise and passion, we aim to inspire the next generation of VR enthusiasts and developers in East Africa.
