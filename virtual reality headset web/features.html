Features & Specifications

Stereo Microphones

Voice is one of the most important elements in a VR headset. Beyond has an array of studio-grade microphones with high-end components and membranes that provide astounding sound quality. This provides high-fidelity audio without plosives, compression, or artifacts in multiplayer games and social VR.

Proximity Sensor

Beyond features a tiny proximity sensor near the nose. This sensor detects when the headset is being worn. When not worn, the displays automatically turn off, preventing screen burn-in and prolonging the life of the headset.

5m Fiber Optic Cable

The headset is powered by a custom, ultra-lightweight 5-meter fiber optic cable that connects to the USB-C port on the left side of the headset. The cable is easy to remove or replace. The cable connects the headset to a Link Box. The Link Box has a DisplayPort cable and two USB 3.0 cables, which connect to the PC.

USB-C Accessory Port

The USB-C port on the right side of the headset connects to Beyond's Audio Strap or other 3rd-party accessories such as USB-C to 3.5mm audio jack adapters and headphones. The port provides power and USB 2.0 data speeds to accessories, and is capable of driving high-end high-impedance headphones.

personalized face cushion

Based on that precise scan, we craft a facial interface just for you. With a perfectly fitting face cushion, there is no more light leakage in VR. This enables ultimate immersion, as you're no longer distracted by light and objects in your surrounding.

Precise Full Body Tracking Support

Body tracking has revolutionized the way users immerse themselves in VRChat, and with Bigscreen Beyond fully supporting all SteamVR trackers, the experience is elevated to new heights. Dive deeper into VRChat with body trackers, allowing you to dance, interact, and express yourself.
